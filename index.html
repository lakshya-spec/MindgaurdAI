About MindGuard AI

MindGuard AI is an offline-first emotional well-being companion. Journal entries are analyzed on-device for emotional tone, with suggestions tailored to how you're feeling. Version 1.0.0 (UI preview) — built to later connect to an on-device AI model and a local SQLite database, so your reflections never have to leave your device.
